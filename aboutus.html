User Friendly. Coming to study in a big city like Pune at such a young age is indeed a daunting experience for any individual. Here, in our college, we have students from all around the state, most of whom are living away from their families for the very first time. Hence, proper provision of basic facilities such as easily available medical care is something of utmost importance. Unfortunately, we discovered that the medical staff assigned to the respective girls' and boys' hostels at PICT arenâ€™t always present at times when the students urgently need them. This was an issue that we thought was necessary to be solved. Therefore, as a part of our project, we decided to create a website that helps students to find doctors near the college. It gives easy and quick access to students in order to help them find the necessary information. It is flexible, and easy to use and is designed and developed to deliver real conceivable benefits to the students.Keeping in mind that the website is to be used in times of an emergency, we made sure to only add medical care services within a 5 km radius.

Our Team

While brainstorming ideas for our project, we came up with several issues around us that we thought could be solved; however most of them seemed very superficial. Eventually, one of our colleagues recalled an intriguing incident that occurred a few months ago when she was suffering from an eye infection, and due to some reason, the respective college medical authority was unavailable at the time. It led to a great deal of confusion and panic considering the fact that she was new to the city and the area was relatively unknown to her. It was very challenging to find a medical center that was within reach and reliable at the same time at the eleventh hour. While it was taken care of at the time, we realized that this was a problem that almost anyone could face at any time and could not be taken lightly. This is what we think inspired us to create this website in order to help our students find easy and quick access to medical facilities as and when required irrespective of the day or time. We have worked extensively to provide a better user experience and made the website much more responsive and user friendly. This project was developed by keeping in the view that the website must be easily accessible and available at any time. This website consists of various filters by which users can easily get the results that they want. General users with basic computer skills can use this website easily. The interface of this website is user-friendly.
